Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2025

DSA Buddy is a privacy-focused Chrome extension designed to help users solve LeetCode problems with smart hints and productivity tools.

What We Collect

  • ✅ We do NOT collect any personal information such as name, email, or browsing history.
  • ✅ We do NOT track users across websites.
  • ✅ All data (like saved notes) stays in your browser using chrome.storage.

Analytics

In future, we may add anonymous analytics (such as how often users click "Get Hint") to improve the extension. This data will never contain any personally identifiable information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

Third-Party APIs

This extension may use external APIs (e.g., Cohere, Gemini) to generate hints. Only the LeetCode problem title/description is sent, not your personal data.
